Welcome to Champaign!

Champaign is a city located in central Illinois with a population of over 80,000 people. The city closely borders another city, Urbana, and both cities are home to the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Champaign has numerous trendy restaurants and experiences to offer to keep the young university population entertained. Whether you are looking to pick apples at the Curtis Orchard, enjoy classy Italian dining, or perhaps something else, the city of Champaign has something to offer for all.
